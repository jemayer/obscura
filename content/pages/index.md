# Obscura

<u>**Obscura** is a purpose-built static site generator for photography portfolio websites.</u> It takes a directory of pictures organised into galleries, blog posts and simple pages formatted in Markdown, and produces a fully static website with responsive images, a lightbox viewer, extracted EXIF data, and cross-referenced content.

**Obscura** is not a general-purpose publishing tool. Every design decision is made in service of one use case: a photographer showcasing their work on the web. For this, you get a fancy CLI tool to support your image metadata workflow on top. <u>We make this less webpack, more f-stop.</u>
